Gravitational Interaction

One interaction of primary importance for the motion of objects near the Earth is the gravitational interaction with the Earth.

Learning Goals

After completing this module you should be able to:

  • Calculate the gravitational forces on two spherical objects from the force law of universal gravitation.
  • Define the parameter g and use it to relate mass and weight.
  • Correctly include the weight acting on an object near the Earth's surface in the object's free body diagram.
