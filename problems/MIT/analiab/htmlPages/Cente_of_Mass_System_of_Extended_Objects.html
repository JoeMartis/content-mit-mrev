The Center of Mass of a System of Extended Objects:

You can encounter a situation where a system constituted by more than one extended object. To find the center of mass of a system of this kind:

  • Replace each of the extended objects by a point particle.
  • The mass of each point particle is the mass of the corresponding extended object.
  • The position of each point particle is the position of the center of mass of the corresponding object.
