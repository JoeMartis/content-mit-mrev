Due Dates and Times

Due dates for each unit are listed on the left-hand navigation bar. All assignments are due just before midnight (UTC) on Sundays, but you can do them all days or even weeks in advance.

Note that the times listed are UTC, or Greenwitch Mean Time, which is probably not your local time zone. You can use this link or this one to convert UTC times into your local time.

Grading

Each problem - that is, each time you see the "Check/Save/Show Answer" boxes - is worth a number of points as indicated. Most problems are worth between 1 and 3 points though some sections of multi-part problems are worth less. Problems without a point total marked are worth 1 point.

Points are only compared within a particular section - homework points and quiz points are averaged separately before being combined into your grade.
